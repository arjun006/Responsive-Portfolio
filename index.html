About Me

I've always enjoyed being creative and adventureous, before I made the decision to try my hand at web development, I would spend my time sketching or trying to find new places and exciting ways to photograph and capture a scene or moment and share it on social media.


Web development gives me a new medium to showcase my creativity, and a new challenge in trying to express an emotion, mood or theme through the design of a webpages and applications. I am especially drawn to the freedom and mobility of web development, the ability to work and be productive almost anywhere in the world and not be confined to a workspace is a luxury most others careers don't have.
